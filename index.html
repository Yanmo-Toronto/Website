About Us

At Yanmo, we are dedicated to bringing the rich Arabic culture and language to children in Toronto through engaging and educational books.

Our mission is to create a bridge between cultures, helping children connect with their heritage or discover a new one.

Founded in 2020, we've been working with local communities, schools, and families to promote cultural understanding and appreciation.
